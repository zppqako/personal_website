About

My days are built on bare-metal code, real-time systems, and hardware that demands absolute precision—a world where logic is the only currency.

Off the screen, I'm on the mountain—chasing speed, reading terrain, moving on instinct.

"I feel deeply, but express clumsily—so I started recording."

The camera became my other language.

No compilation errors. No edge cases.
Just the warmth of light, the stillness between moments, and emotions that need no comment blocks.

I build with logic.
I ski on instinct.
I capture with light.

— Bill
